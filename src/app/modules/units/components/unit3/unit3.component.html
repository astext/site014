Made with Tunafish & VSTs

32 bit software, 32 bit PCs

Around 160 pieces were conceived. Not every one was successful, but I am still pleased by half of them. Some of the tracks below are also available on SoundCloud as pre 2018 lower quality mixes. The versions below are newly reworked in 320kbps mp3. I used Tunafish and freeware VST synths for all tracks, before Ableton was available. Tunafish is made by the creator of Moonfish and TU2/Tuareg also fascinating and worthwhile digital tools. The high version numbers are partly due to the fact that Tunafish crashed often, so I had to be very proactive in saving work. Or because I really did hammer them that many times! I have included the rough with the smooth here to memorialise the primitive tools and the imperfections of my musical skills at the time.
